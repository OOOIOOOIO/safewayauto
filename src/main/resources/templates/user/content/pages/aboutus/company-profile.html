AJ International Traders is an export oriented company of used vehicles and auto parts. We are offering car purchase solution to used car exporters and customers around the globe to buy cars at ease.

Since our establishment in 2010, our business has been experiencing 100% sales growth each year. In 2012, we were proud to be a recipient of President Award for exporting 10 million dollars worth goods overseas. In 2015, we have exported 2500 units to 21 countries with $20 million dollars sales revenue.

Along with AJ Rentacar and AJ Sellcar, AJ International Traders is a subsidiary company of AJ Family Corporation. For your guidance, AJ Rentacar is one of leading rentacar business in Korea with 632 billion dollars sales revenue in 2015. AJ Sellcar owns the biggest auto auction in Korea and has purchasing departments across Korea. Therefore, we are able to source big volumes of rent cars and personal cars that have undergone proper maintenance and strict inspection through our networks.

The advantage of doing business with AJ International Traders is that we offer 100% transparent accident history for all of our vehicles at domestic market price. Through our “Auction” service, we encourage overseas buyers to find their favorite vehicles and to participate in bidding with their desired price. Also, we offer “All Inclusive Concierge Service” where we assist buyers step by step from helping buyers to find their favorite vehicles and to deliver their vehicles to their designated countries.

Under our slogan “What You See is What You Get”, we are endlessly pursuing R&D to make 100% transparent transaction with overseas buyers and to build stronger trust between both parties for our mutual benefits.
